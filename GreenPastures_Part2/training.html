Training Packages

Full-time Training

Horses in part-time training are worked by our trainers five times a week, with goals tailored to the owner. Fulltime training includes full-board

Part-time Training

Horses in part-time training will be worked 3 times a week. Part-time training can be packaged with full or packaged with full or partial care board.

Individual Training Sessions

Individual training sessions are available for both boarder and clients who would like to haul in. Individual training sessions are not packeged with board.
